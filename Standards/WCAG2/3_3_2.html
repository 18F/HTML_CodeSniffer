Success Criterion 3.3.2: Labels or Instructions (Level A)

Labels or instructions are provided when content requires user input.

(From Understanding WCAG 2.0: Understanding Success Criterion 3.3.2)

Discussion

On providing labels and instructions

Most of the testing of labels are tested in Success Criterion 1.3.1. However, general instructions that cannot be marked up using labels - eg. for each form, section or fieldset - also help to fulfil this Success Criterion.
